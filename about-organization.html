University Student Supreme Government

University Student Supreme Government (USSG) is the head or mother of all the organizations of the University of Rizal System (URS). This organization comprises of student officers from different courses and year levels standing as the governing leaders for the campus. Together with the help of their adviser, the Office of the Student Development and Services (OSDS), the faculty members and students, they create and execute many various programs and activities, that profoundly enrich the welfare of all the stake-holders. USSG functions as the students' voice: they seek and provide the needs of the students by means of constructive seminars, empowering contests, environmental cleaning, cohesive social gatherings, and other more. The USSG aims for the students to embrace the URS's Core Values: Responsiveness, Integrity, Service, Excellence, and Social Responsibility; thus, they become the tomorrow's noblest.

URSC-VOLUNTEERS Organization

The members of URSC-VOLUNTEERS Organization act as advocates to care for the environment. They also develop and conduct well-benefited sustainable development programs, projects, and activities for the community. This Organization promotes camaraderie, social awareness, and uphold good relationship with other students and faculty. Some of the activities undertaken by the URSC-VOLUNTEERS includes outreach programs for the less fortunate members of the community, Bloodletting activity and Environmental protection and even facilitating in Disaster Risk Reduction Management trainings and other seminars for student development.
