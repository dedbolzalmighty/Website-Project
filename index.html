Back to Face-to-Face

Face-to-face classes might be limited to only a few hours at a time, should President Rodrigo Duterte approve their pilot run in areas with low risk of COVID-19 transmission. He also said that Face-to-face classes do not mean 8 hours, 5 days a week. These could be held an hour per week, 3 hours per week, in a combination of module, computer-aided learning and face-to-face classes if students need to coordinate with teachers.

Covid - Free

Schools need anti-covid protection if the face to face classes are back. Putting alcohol before entering the school is required all of the universities security guards make sure that all of the students , teachers and other staff use alcohol to sanitize their hand before entering the school. All must need to put facemask and face shield, less physical contact and maintain social distancing.
